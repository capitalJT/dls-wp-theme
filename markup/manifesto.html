The Ambition for the New Al Jazeera

Create a truly contemporary news channel, aware of its relationship to digital media and aware of its audience’s changing habit, while remaining true to a noble tradition of journalism. Emphasize the content produced at AJ because today more than ever, content is king. Hence the need to ensure that engaging with the content is effortless and enjoyable.

A Design at the service of information

Today, we’re flooded with a massive amount of data and we strongly believe that in order to manage complexity, there’s nothing as powerful as simplicity.

A design that concentrates on readability and navigability, aims at creating the best possible viewing experience.

  • Inform rather than decorate.
  • The power of simplicity.

Al Jazeera is the window to the world

It is a portal offered to the viewers to access all parts of the world.

A Strong editorial philosophy

A channel which challenges perception, inspires the audiences and enhances the understanding of what matters to them.

A News hub for the digital age

A consistent visual identity that gives strong foundations for designing across all platforms : the new on-air design for AJ goes far beyond the TV set and becomes the foundation of an identity project with more scope.

A Whole system, not just a logo

The screens and their constant user interactions have brought the need for new branding systems and vocabularies. Great contemporary brands, especially leading digital ones, deploy a full in-depth brand identity, within which the logo is a part of a bigger system that has a powerful look & feel.
